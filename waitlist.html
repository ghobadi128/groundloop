Coming soon to your neighborhood —

Your café's coffee grounds could be growing someone's mushrooms by Saturday.

GroundLoop is a live local map connecting coffee shops with the gardeners, composters, mushroom growers, schools, and artists who want their used grounds. Free pickup for shops. Free material for growers. Nothing good goes to landfill.

40 lbs — what a single busy café discards in coffee grounds every day. Multiply by every café on your street.

How it will work

If you grow things —

Open the map, see what's free near you right now, reserve it, pick it up (or have it delivered). Your first month is free; after that it's $6 a month — less than one latte.

If you run a café, brewery, or garden center —

Post your surplus in thirty seconds. A neighbor collects it on your schedule. You get less trash and a sustainability report worth bragging about. Free, forever.
